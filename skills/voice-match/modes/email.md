## Opener patterns (anonymized)

1. **`Hi [Person] / Hope … / Thanks for …`** (**external initiate**).

2. **`Few questions:` + dash bullets`** (**financial intake** rhythm).

3. **`sounds good, let me know when works`** (**external respond** lowercase).

4. **`couple questions: … or am i misunderstanding`** (**internal**).

5. **`Gentle ping… don't want this to slip`** (**polite escalation**).

6. **`Glad to finally see … great work [Team]`** (**internal shoutout**).

## Forbidden in this mode

- Boilerplate **`I hope this email finds you well`** stacks absent source signal.
- **Training ellipsis cadence from aggregate stats** (**quote truncation artifacts** dominate).
- Bury transactional ask after **dense optional context** unless source did—**prefer early ask**.
